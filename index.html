Places to discover

Praia da Amoreira

This is a pet-friendly beach located in the city of Santa Cruz. Among several beaches in the region, this one is exclusive for pets. The beach is very popular with surfers and other water sports enthusiasts, but it also offers quiet spaces for walks and leisure time.

Parque Canino Alameda Keil do Amaral

This space is dedicated to dog lovers, with an area of ​​10,000 m² where pets can play and exercise freely. The park also offers tables for humans to have picnics and a skate park for extra fun.

Praia do Barranquinho

A paradise for you and your best friend, this is the first official dog beach in Portugal! Located in the Lagoa region of the Algarve, this extensive area of ​​around 80 meters offers a safe and exclusive space for your dog to have fun in the sea and on the sand.
